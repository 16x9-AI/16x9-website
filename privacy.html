Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 16x9 ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at 16x9.ai. We are committed to handling your information transparently and in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information we collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you provide directly: when you fill out the contact form on our site, you give us your name, email address, the role you identify with (enterprise, founder, advisor, investor, other), and a message describing what you are exploring with us.
  • Information collected automatically: when you visit our site, our analytics tools collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referring source, country and approximate region, and interactions with the page (clicks, scrolls, form submissions). This data is collected only after you provide consent through our cookie banner.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and follow up on partnership conversations.
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve the user experience and content.
  • Monitor site performance and detect technical errors.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it for automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

3. Cookies and analytics

We use the following analytics tools, which are loaded only after you accept cookies through our consent banner:

  • Microsoft Clarity: provides heatmaps and session recordings to help us understand how visitors navigate the site. Clarity may set cookies on your device. Sensitive content (such as form input fields) is masked by default. Microsoft Privacy Statement.
  • Google Analytics 4: provides aggregated metrics about traffic, sessions, and conversions. We have enabled IP anonymization. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Identify.id: a business-to-business visitor identification service that may match visiting IP addresses to publicly available company information so we can understand which organizations are interested in our work. We do not receive personal contact details of individual visitors through this service. Identify.id Privacy Policy.

You can change your cookie choice at any time by clearing your site data or contacting us at info@16x9.ai.

4. Data sharing

We share information only with the third-party processors listed in section 3 (Microsoft, Google, Identify.id), strictly to operate the analytics and visitor-identification functions described. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone for marketing purposes.

5. Data retention

We retain enquiry information (name, email, message) only as long as necessary to maintain the conversation with you, or until you ask us to delete it. Analytics data is retained according to the default retention periods set by Microsoft Clarity, Google Analytics, and Identify.id, which we may shorten at our discretion.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw your consent to analytics tracking at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@16x9.ai.

7. International transfers

16x9 operates internationally. The third-party processors listed in section 3 may transfer and process data outside your country of residence, including in the United States. These transfers are governed by the privacy frameworks of each processor.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all site traffic. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work continuously to protect the information we receive.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at info@16x9.ai.
